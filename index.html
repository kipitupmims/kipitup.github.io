About Kipitup

Kipitup is an Apple Watch app that provides fitness advice for any athlete, no matter where they are on their journey.

It incorporates both the wearer's biosensory data and industry leading best practices powered by an Al-based recommendation system to ensure that advice is always specific to what would be best for the wearer.

All you need to do is ask: "I am running a marathon in 10 weeks. Suggest me a training routine."

Core Features

Personalized Training Routines

Tailored advice for marathon preparation and other fitness goals

Biosensory Data Utilization

Understanding your body vitals before sharing crucial advice

Interactive Prompts

Voice-activated fitness advice directly from your Apple Watch

Question Versatility

Kipitup will intelligently respond to any type of question posted
